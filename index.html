Eliza Taylor

Eliza Taylor portrays Clarke Griffin, the protagonist in "The 100." Clarke is a resourceful and determined leader who often finds herself making tough decisions for the survival of her people. Eliza's compelling performance has been central to the series' success, showcasing her ability to convey strength, vulnerability, and resilience.


Bob Morley

Bob Morley plays Bellamy Blake, a central character known for his evolution from a rebellious youth to a formidable leader. Bellamy's journey is marked by his deep loyalty and protective nature towards his sister and his people. Bob's portrayal of Bellamy has captivated audiences, highlighting his complex and dynamic character arc.


Marie Avgeropoulos

Marie Avgeropoulos brings to life Octavia Blake, Bellamy's younger sister. Octavia's transformation from a hidden child to a fierce warrior, known as "Skairipa" and later as a leader, is one of the most compelling arcs in the series. Marie's performance captures Octavia's strength, independence, and struggle for identity.


Lindsey Morgan

Lindsey Morgan portrays Raven Reyes, a brilliant mechanic and engineer who plays a crucial role in the survival of the group. Raven's intelligence, determination, and resilience shine through in every episode, making her one of the most beloved characters. Lindsey's nuanced performance brings depth to Raven's character, showcasing her vulnerabilities and strengths.


Richard Harmon

Richard Harmon takes on the role of John Murphy, a character who starts as an antagonist but evolves into a complex anti-hero. Murphy's journey from a troubled youth to a trusted ally is marked by his sharp wit and survival instincts. Richard's portrayal adds layers to Murphy, making him one of the most intriguing characters in the series.
